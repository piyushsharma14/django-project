Vegetable Burgers Recipe

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 40 mins
Rating

01.

Heat oil in a pan and add all the beans, carrots, cauliflower and cabbage to it. Stir over high heat.Add coriander powder, salt, turmeric powder and mix well.

02.

Turn off the heat. Keep aside to cool.When cool mix in the boiled potatoes and peas.Make thin rounds so as to match the bun.Dust with maida and dip into the beaten egg.

03.

Coat with bread crumbs.Heat oil and deep fry till they turn golden brown in colour.Slit the burger roll and place the patty in it along with mustard and mayonnaise.
