Bajra-Methi Missi Roti Recipe

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 25 mins
Rating

How to Make Bajra-Methi Missi Roti

1.Combine all the ingredients and knead into firm dough with required amount of water.
2.Divide into five portions. Roll out into thin rotis and roast on a tawa till they are well done.
3.Serve hot with green chutney.

Key Ingredients

Bajra flour, Wheat flour, Fenugreek leaves, Garlic cloves, Low fat curd, Green chilli, Low fat paneer, Coriander leaves, Haldi powder, Red chilli powder, Salt
