Grilled Veg Pesto Sandwich Recipe

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 1 hour 30 mins
Rating

01.

Cut all the bell peppers, zucchini, onion, garlic and mushrooms.Heat oil in a pan add the garlic, onion, bell peppers and toss it well. Now add the mushrooms and zucchini and toss is again.

02.

Add the pesto sauce and white sauce and mix well. Add the cheese and give it a nice stir. Add salt as per your taste and give it a good mix.Remove the mixture from the heat and keep aside. Add mayonnaise and celery. Mix it well.

03.

Now toast or grill the bread slices on a pan. Apply butter to it and the mixture.Add butter to the second slice as well and place it over, completing a sandwich.Serve it with a side salad and French Fries

Key Ingredients

Yellow bell pepper, Green bell pepper, Red bell pepper, Green zucchini , Red zucchini, Tomato, Pesto sauce, Cheese, White sauce, Salt, Garlic, Onion, Celery, Oil, Bread, Butter, Mayonnaise, Mushroom, Basil leaves, Cashew nuts, Garlic, Parmesan cheese, Salt and oil
